About Our Choir

Our History

Founded in 2005, Elmshaven Choir began as a small group of passionate singers in a local church. Over the years, we’ve grown into a vibrant choir of more than 30 dedicated members.


Our journey has taken us from humble beginnings to performing in renowned concert halls, but our mission remains unchanged: to share the joy, beauty, and message of choral music with all.

Rooted in faith and united by music, we are committed to touching hearts, inspiring souls, and connecting people to Jesus through every note we sing.

Meet Our Conductor

Mr Jean De Deui NIYONKURU

Over 20 years of commitment and experience within our choir family, Mr. Jean De Deui brings deep knowledge, creativity, and heartfelt passion to Elmshaven Choir. As the founder and current president, he was there from the very beginning, helping form what started as a small group of dedicated singers in a local church.

His leadership and innovative approach to choral music have shaped the choir’s sound and spirit, inspiring members to grow musically and spiritually. Through his guidance, Elmshaven Choir continues its mission to share the love of God and the beauty of music with every performance.
